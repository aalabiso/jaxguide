The Essentials
🚗
Parking
Pull into the driveway between 5411 (little house) and 5401 (big house). Park in the spot closest to 5411 and leave room for other vehicles to enter and exit at the main house.
🧺
Laundry
The washer is just outside the exterior bathroom door, to the left. The key is hanging inside the bathroom. Detergent and supplies are stored under the bathroom sink.
🚽
Plumbing
Please only flush toilet paper — no wipes, paper towels, or anything else, even if labeled "flushable." Our pipes are a bit sensitive and we want everything running smoothly for you!
🔑
Lockup
Please lock the door when you leave and when you go to bed. Check-in details and door codes are provided in your booking confirmation message.
🚭

Strictly No Smoking or Vaping. Smoking or vaping of any kind — including cigarettes, e-cigarettes, vaping devices, and cannabis — is prohibited inside the guest house and on the property. Violation will result in a $250 deep cleaning fee to restore the space for future guests.

💡 Good to Know

We're just next door at the main house — so if you need absolutely anything or have any questions during your stay, don't hesitate to reach out. We're happy to help with recommendations, directions, or anything at all.

😊 That's it! No long list of rules here. We just ask that you treat the place like your own, have a wonderful time, and enjoy everything Jacksonville has to offer. We're so glad to have you.
