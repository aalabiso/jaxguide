☀️ March Weather

One of the best months to visit Jacksonville. Warm days in the mid-70s, cool nights in the low 60s, low humidity, and minimal rain. Perfect for outdoor dining, beach days, golf, and exploring the city on foot. Pack layers for evenings.

🐳 Right Whale Season (Final Weeks): North Atlantic right whales — one of Earth's rarest species with fewer than 350 remaining — are still spotted off Jacksonville's coast through March. Watch from Jax Beach or book a boat tour. · 🌺 Ravine Gardens Azalea Trail in Palatka (1 hr south) hits peak bloom mid-March. 100,000+ azaleas line dramatic ravine trails. $6 park entry — stunning photo ops.

🍽️ March Dining Tip

THE PLAYERS Championship week transforms Ponte Vedra into a foodie destination. Many top Jax restaurants set up pop-ups at the Taste of Jax Village inside the tournament gates. It's a great way to sample the city's best chefs in one spot — with world-class golf as the backdrop. Pro tip: the Gate River Run after-party on March 7 is a massive downtown celebration worth attending even if you don't run.
