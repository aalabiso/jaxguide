☀️ APRIL WEATHER

April is Jacksonville's sweet spot. Warm days in the low 80s, cool evenings around 60°F, low humidity, and mostly sunny skies. Perfect for beach days, outdoor dining, golf, and festival-going. Light layers for evenings, sunscreen for days.

🍽️ APRIL DINING TIP

Springing The Blues weekend transforms Jacksonville Beach into a food destination. Beyond the festival vendors, check out the restaurants along 1st Street and Beach Boulevard — many run special menus. Don't miss Safe Harbor Seafood for the freshest catch in town, or grab fish tacos at Taco Lu on the beach.
